### Description (4000 characters max)

Chaki is a comprehensive expedition planning app built for adventurers who venture into remote and challenging environments. Whether you're organizing a multi-week trek through the Brooks Range, a high-altitude expedition in Peru, or a river trip in the backcountry, Chaki consolidates your entire planning workflow into one place.

ITINERARY PLANNING
Build detailed day-by-day itineraries with elevation tracking and acclimatization visualization. Plan route segments, identify water sources, and map your journey with integrated MapKit support.

GEAR MANAGEMENT
Organize gear by category with weight tracking and pack status. Mark items as Critical, Suggested, Optional, or Contingent. Track total pack weight and ensure nothing gets left behind.

TEAM COORDINATION
Manage participants with group assignments, flight details, and contact information. Keep emergency contacts and local resource contacts organized and accessible.

LOGISTICS
- Transport legs: flights, buses, boats, and overland segments
- Accommodation: hotels, campsites, huts, and lodging at waypoints
- Resupply points: post offices, local contacts, and services
- Permits and travel documents with deadline tracking
- Satellite communicator and tracking device details

BUDGET TRACKING
Track expenses across multiple currencies with real-time conversion. Categorize costs and view budget summaries to stay on track.

SAFETY PLANNING
Document risk assessments with hazard identification and mitigation strategies. Store insurance policy details and emergency evacuation plans. Reference historical climate data for route planning.

PDF GUIDEBOOK EXPORT
Generate comprehensive PDF guidebooks from your expedition data — perfect for sharing with team members, emergency contacts, or printing as a backup reference.

CLOUDKIT SYNC
All your expedition data syncs seamlessly across your iPhone and iPad via iCloud, so your plans are always up to date.

CSV IMPORT
Import gear lists, itineraries, and other data from spreadsheets to quickly populate your expedition plans.

Chaki is designed exclusively for pre-trip planning — helping you prepare thoroughly before you head into the field.
